Myers-Briggs Type Indicator

           MBTI stands for Myers-Briggs-Type-Indicator and it was constructed by Katharine Cook Briggs and her daughter Isabel Briggs Myers with the help of Carl Jung's theories. The MBTI questionnaire were first published in the U.S 1943 by them. They developed more accurate question for more detailed results till 1962. They actually made those for whole 20 years. And starting from 2020, MBTI is getting more and more attention. Especially among teenagers and GenZs.


What is Mbti?

            MBTI is a tool which is frequently used to help individuals understand their own communication preference and how they interact with others. Having an awareness of what MBTI is can help you adapt your interpersonal approach to different situations and audiences. There are 16 types of personalities in total. The table below shows the 16 types.
