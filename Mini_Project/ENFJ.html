Protagonist

ENFJs

            People with this personality type are outgoing, compassionate and natural leaders. They are known as protagonists. These individuals are known for their ability to inspire others and create positive change in the world. Considered to be natural-born leaders, ENFJs are often the life of the party and excel in social settings. People with this personality type are devoted altruists, ready to face slings and arrows in order to stand up for the people and ideas that they believe in. Protagonists are born leaders, which explains why these personalities can be found among many notable politicians, coaches, and teachers.
